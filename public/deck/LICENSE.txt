Tarot card face images — Rider-Waite-Smith deck
=================================================

These 78 card images depict the Rider-Waite-Smith tarot deck, originally
published 1909/1910 (artwork by Pamela Colman Smith), which is in the PUBLIC
DOMAIN in the United States.

Source of this particular image set: metabismuth/tarot-json
  https://github.com/metabismuth/tarot-json  (code MIT; images noted PD in US)
Files were renamed to this app's stable imageKey scheme:
  major_00.jpg .. major_21.jpg
  <suit>_<rank>.jpg  with suit ∈ {wands,cups,swords,pentacles},
                          rank ∈ {01..10, page, knight, queen, king}
